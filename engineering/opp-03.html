Digital Jewels Scholarship 2026 for Nigerian Students

Deadline: August 17, 2026

About the scholarship

The DJ Cares Scholarship is organized by Digital Jewels Africa with a vision of becoming Africa's trusted guide in the emerging knowledge economy by providing high-quality training, e-learning, and specialized recruitment services. Through the DJ Cares Scholarship, Digital Jewels Africa seeks to provide career guidance to enable experienced and inexperienced professionals to develop skills relevant to the information age.

This scholarship is part of the DJ Cares initiative, aimed at reducing the number of students at risk of dropping out due to financial constraints and encouraging them to excel academically.

Overview

  • Type: Undergraduate Scholarships
  • Eligible countries: Nigeria
  • Reward: Full Scholarship + ₦200,000 per year
  • Deadline: August 17, 2026

Eligibility criteria

  • Be a Nigerian.
  • Be studying Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, Mechatronics, or Electrical Engineering.
  • Have a minimum 3.5/5.0 CGPA (or equivalent).
  • Be currently in your 2nd or 3rd year of study.
  • Be enrolled full-time in an accredited Federal or State university.
  • Show clear demonstration of scholarship requirements.

Application documents

Students who meet the eligibility criteria must write and submit an essay of no more than 500 words on one of the three topics below:

  • Balancing innovation and security: the ethical dilemma of AI-enabled deepfakes in information technology and what it means for information security.
  • Tech literacy and its importance in the 21st century: analyze the importance of tech literacy today and how it impacts personal growth and opportunities.
  • My digital footprint: understanding the impact of my online presence — reflect on your online activities and how your digital footprint can affect your future education and career prospects.

Note: the file must be in PDF format.

Application guidelines

  • Click the "Apply Now" button below and proceed to fill the application form.
  • Students who have successfully written their essays will continue the application process by clicking "Apply" to fill in their data and upload essays.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be contacted and notified.
