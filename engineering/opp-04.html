E.A. Adeboye Tech Fellowship 2026

Applications open now — free, 4-month virtual fellowship

Description

Applications are now open for the E.A. Adeboye Tech Fellowship 2026, a free four-month virtual fellowship designed to equip young people with practical digital skills, leadership capacity, innovation experience, and mentorship for career growth and global opportunities.

Delivered by The Incubator Hub, the fellowship combines structured learning, project-based training, and mentorship to help participants develop real-world skills that prepare them for careers in technology, innovation, and leadership.

About the Fellowship

The E.A. Adeboye Tech Fellowship is a structured development programme that helps young people grow into leaders, innovators, and technology builders. Participants receive practical training, mentorship, and hands-on project experience designed to develop skills that can be applied in the workplace, entrepreneurship, and community development.

The programme welcomes beginners as well as aspiring technology professionals who are ready to learn, build, and make meaningful impact. Participants who successfully complete all programme requirements will receive a certificate of completion.

About The Incubator Hub

The Incubator Hub is a technology and innovation organisation dedicated to developing the next generation of African leaders through practical training, mentorship, innovation programmes, and community-driven learning experiences. The organisation designs and delivers the fellowship curriculum, workshops, mentorship sessions, and project-based learning activities throughout the programme.

Programme Details

  • Programme: E.A. Adeboye Tech Fellowship
  • Duration: 4 Months
  • Learning Format: Virtual
  • Programme Fee: Free
  • Eligible Age: 16–35 years
  • Certification: Awarded upon successful completion

Training Areas

Participants will receive practical training in:

  • Software Development
  • Data Analysis
  • Cybersecurity
  • Project Management
  • Product Management
  • Innovation and Leadership
  • Data Science
  • AI Engineering
  • AI Automation
