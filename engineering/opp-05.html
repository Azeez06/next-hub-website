PSU International Internship Inbound Scholarship Program 2026 (Thailand)

Deadline: Not mentioned — Apply Early

Overview

Applications are now open for the PSU International Internship Inbound Scholarship Program 2026. The programme offers international undergraduate and graduate students the opportunity to undertake a fully funded six-week internship at Prince of Songkla University (PSU), Thailand.

Participants will gain practical research and professional experience while working alongside faculty members in their selected departments. The programme also promotes international collaboration, cultural exchange, and academic development.

Programme Details

  • Programme: PSU International Internship Inbound Scholarship Program 2026
  • Host Institution: Prince of Songkla University (PSU), Thailand
  • Programme Type: Scholarship / International Internship
  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Internship Dates: Determined by the intern and host department
  • Application Deadline: Not mentioned

Internship Areas

Interns may be placed in one of the following faculties or departments:

  • Faculty of Science
  • Faculty of Medicine
  • PSU International College
  • Faculty of Nursing
  • Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences
  • Faculty of Environmental Management
  • Faculty of Veterinary Science
  • Faculty of Political Science
  • Faculty of International Studies
  • Faculty of Hospitality and Tourism
  • Faculty of Technology and Environment
  • Prince of Songkla University Trang Campus
  • Faculty of Engineering

Eligibility

  • Open to international students from all countries.
  • Must be currently enrolled at an accredited university outside Thailand.
  • Open to undergraduate and graduate students.
  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.25 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Strong English language proficiency is required.
  • Must submit a confirmation letter from the current university.
  • Interns must submit a Student's Internship Report after completing the programme.

Scholarship Benefits

  • 20,000 Baht scholarship
  • International economy-class airfare
  • Accommodation
  • Daily living allowance
  • Health and accident insurance
  • Passport and visa fees
  • Other internship-related expenses
