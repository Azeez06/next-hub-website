Who we are

Next Hub is a WhatsApp-based career community built for Nigerian students and graduates. We exist because too many talented, hardworking students graduate with good grades and zero experience, not for lack of ability, but for lack of access to the opportunities and guidance that were available to them all along.

What we do

Every day, we curate verified jobs, internships, and fellowships and deliver them directly to our members — organized by faculty, so the opportunities are always relevant, never noise. Beyond listings, we help members build a real digital career presence: CV and cover letter support, LinkedIn and Indeed profile optimization, and a writing incentive program that rewards members for building their personal brand online.

Who we serve

Three realities we see in Nigerian undergraduates and graduates every day.

01

The passive student

Comes to school, does assignments and exams, does IT, does the project, graduates. Never knows virtual jobs, fellowships, and internships exist. Their broad course prepares them for exams, not for a career.

Result: zero experience by graduation.
02

The unguided student

Knows opportunities exist, but with no direction, chases everything at once studying one course while learning an unrelated skill because "that's where the money is." Graduates with many half-finished skills and no real foundation.

Result: many skills, no foundation, no positioning.
03

The unconnected student

Knows what they want and has real direction, but doesn't know who can actually help them get there as there is no one guiding their CV, their LinkedIn, or their applications.

Result: effort with no access. This is where Next Hub begins.

Our commitment

Decent Work and Economic Growth

Next Hub's work directly supports UN Sustainable Development Goal 8 — specifically Target 8.6, which calls for substantially reducing the proportion of young people not in employment, education, or training. By connecting Nigerian students and graduates to verified, relevant opportunities and equipping them with the tools to compete for them, Next Hub works daily to close the gap between graduation and gainful employment — one student, one opportunity at a time.
