Youth Vanguard for Education Fellowship Program 2026

Deadline: 31 July 2026

Description

Are you a young, passionate Nigerian eager to advance education reform in Nigeria? QEDA, in partnership with the Federal Ministry of Youth Development, Unveiling Africa and SEED Care & Support Foundation, is excited to announce the Call for Applications for the Youth Vanguards for Education (YVE) Fellowship Program.

The YVE Fellowship is a six-month program designed to empower young Nigerians to drive impactful reform in the field of education. Fellows will be equipped with evidence-based knowledge, skills, resources and opportunities that enable them to make informed decisions about their roles as agents of education development in Nigeria — especially through advocacy and innovation — to gain first-hand experience.

Through a 2-month comprehensive virtual training and a school immersion internship, fellows will become equipped to drive education reform at scale. This fellowship aims to bridge demand and supply gaps by empowering a generation of young change-makers with the knowledge, skills and opportunities to advocate for education reform in Nigeria.

Overview

  • Type: Fellowship
  • Sponsor: Federal Government of Nigeria + QEDA
  • Duration: 6 months
  • Benefits: Comprehensive virtual training, school immersion internship, etc.
  • Deadline: 31st July, 2026

Eligibility

  • Availability to fully engage in all aspects of the program (6 hours of online sessions per week), including a full-term school immersion program.
  • Demonstrated interest and commitment towards education reform.
  • Strong commitment to hands-on leadership and career advancement.
  • Great communication and interpersonal skills.
