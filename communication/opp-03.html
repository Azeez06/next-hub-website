2027 Shell Assessed Internship Program

Deadline: Not specified

About the programme

Join the Shell Assessed Internships and be part of the future of global energy. Shell Internships offer opportunities across Technical and Commercial areas — including Downstream, Information Digital Technologies, Finance, and other business areas — based on your study background, skills, and preference.

The Shell Assessed Internship assigns a supervisor and mentor to successful applicants, with regular assessments throughout the internship to ensure you get the most from the experience. You'll have a formal mid-term review with your supervisor and mentor, and at the close of the internship, you'll prepare a presentation about your project with discussion on project delivery and performance.

Highlights

  • Type: Internships
  • Sponsored by: Shell Plc
  • Eligible countries: All countries
  • Benefits: Monthly stipends, hands-on training, mentorship, professional development, health benefits
  • Deadline: Not specified

Eligibility

  • Enjoy being challenged and welcome the opportunity to be innovative.
  • Good at absorbing information, analysing problems, making objective decisions, and coming up with original ideas.
  • Have the drive and resilience to get things done, the flexibility to work well as part of a team, and the credibility to influence others.
  • Be an actively enrolled student who will complete at least one more semester of education following the internship, or be in your penultimate year of study.
