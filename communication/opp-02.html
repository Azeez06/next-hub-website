About the programme

The Women Environmental Programme (WEP) is inviting applications for its 3-Month Digital Skills Development Programme under the WomenPower2030: Feminist Accelerate Action for Sustainable Development Project. This fully funded initiative is designed to equip young girls, women, and persons with disabilities with practical digital and ICT skills that enhance employability, entrepreneurship, and participation in the digital economy.

About the WomenPower2030 project

WomenPower2030 is a Financial Framework Partnership Agreement (FFPA) between the European Union and a consortium of five feminist organizations led by Women Engage for a Common Future (WECF). The initiative seeks to strengthen the capacity of feminist civil society organizations, promote gender-equal sustainable development, influence policies that advance women's rights, and mobilize women, youth, and gender-diverse activists to drive collective action for sustainable development. WEP is one of the implementing partners of this global project.

Programme details

  • Training location: Abuja, Federal Capital Territory (FCT)
  • Duration: 12 weeks (3 months)
  • Cost: Free
  • Slots: Limited

Courses available

  • Office Suite Essentials — Build a strong foundation in computer literacy using Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, along with digital file management and workplace productivity skills.
  • Graphic Design — Learn visual design fundamentals using Canva, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop, and build a portfolio for employment or freelance work.
  • Video Editing — Learn video planning, editing, storytelling, and visual communication using beginner-friendly editing software.
  • UI/UX Design — An introduction to UI/UX design using Figma, covering wireframing, prototyping, user journey mapping, and accessibility.
  • Web Development with WordPress CMS — Learn website creation using WordPress, covering setup, theme customization, plugin integration, and site management.

Eligibility

  • Be a young girl, woman, or person with a disability.
  • Be between 15 and 35 years of age.
  • Have a genuine interest in digital skills and ICT.
  • Be a resident of the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), Abuja.
  • Be available and committed to completing the entire 12-week training programme.
