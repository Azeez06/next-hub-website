Ikore Communications & Media Manager Recruitment 2026

Deadline: 31 July 2026

Description

Are you passionate about storytelling, strategic communications, advocacy, and driving meaningful impact? Ikore is seeking a talented Communications & Media Manager to join its team and help transform Nigeria's agri-food systems and development landscape.

This is an exciting opportunity to lead strategic communication initiatives, amplify impact stories, and engage key stakeholders in support of inclusive, market-driven solutions that improve livelihoods across Sub-Saharan Africa. If you're a creative communicator with a passion for development and social impact, this role is for you.

Job Details

  • Location: Abuja, Nigeria
  • Employment Type: Full-time (Onsite)
  • Application Deadline: 31st July, 2026

Role Overview

As the Communications & Media Manager, you will:

  • Lead and implement media, public relations, and digital communication strategies.
  • Develop compelling content that showcases Ikore's impact and success stories.
  • Drive advocacy campaigns and strengthen stakeholder engagement.
  • Manage the organization's brand visibility across digital and traditional media platforms.
  • Support communication and visibility efforts for development programmes and strategic initiatives.

Why Join Ikore?

  • Work on impactful projects that advance agriculture and sustainable development.
  • Be part of an innovative, mission-driven organization committed to creating lasting change.
  • Contribute to inclusive economic growth and improved livelihoods across Africa.
  • Help shape powerful narratives that influence policy, partnerships, and real-world impact.
  • Collaborate with a passionate team dedicated to transforming communities.
