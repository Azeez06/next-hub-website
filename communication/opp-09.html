AI Fact-Checking Assistant at Gulf Associates

Fully Remote · Flexible Hours

Job Overview

Gulf Associates is seeking an AI Fact-Checking Assistant to review AI-generated research, verify facts using official sources, identify errors or inconsistencies, and help maintain accurate records for commercial advisory work.

In this role, you will play a critical part in ensuring that research and business information are accurate, reliable, and properly verified before reaching senior decision-makers.

Key Responsibilities

  • Review AI-generated research for factual errors and logic gaps.
  • Verify names, dates, and business information using public records and official registries.
  • Cross-check commercial data with original source documents.
  • Ensure research notes and citations are accurate.
  • Flag inconsistencies in reports and maintain verified internal data.

Requirements

Candidates should have:

  • Excellent attention to detail.
  • Strong analytical and research skills.
  • Clear written communication skills.
  • Experience using digital research tools.
  • The ability to work independently in a remote environment.
  • A background in business, law, or the humanities is an added advantage.

Benefits

  • Fully remote work with flexible hours.
  • Competitive pay.
  • Practical experience in commercial advisory and risk assessment.
  • Opportunity to work closely with senior professionals on high-impact projects.

If you're passionate about accuracy, critical thinking, and research, this role offers an excellent opportunity to build valuable experience while working from anywhere.
