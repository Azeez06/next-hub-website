NOBZO Paid Internship Programme 2026

Application Status: Open

Description

Applications are now open for the NOBZO Internship Programme 2026, a three-month paid internship designed to give students, graduates, and early-career professionals practical experience while working on live projects in a growing PropTech company.

Selected interns will become active members of the NOBZO team, collaborate with professionals, solve real business challenges, and build relevant skills across technology, operations, business, finance, human resources, marketing, and creative roles.

About NOBZO

NOBZO is a PropTech company developing innovative solutions for the property and real estate ecosystem. The company uses technology to support how people find, connect with, and manage opportunities within the property market.

Through this internship programme, NOBZO is creating an opportunity for emerging professionals to gain workplace experience while contributing to the company's projects and operations.
